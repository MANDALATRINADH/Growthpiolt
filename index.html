🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use GrowthPilot, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and account credentials when you register.
  • Usage Data: Your daily habits, schedule entries, roadmap progress, notes, journal entries, and learning activities within the app.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, and IP address for security and analytics purposes.
  • Payment Data: When you subscribe to Premium, Razorpay processes your payment. We receive payment confirmation but NEVER store your full credit card, UPI, or banking details.
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our Contact Us form or feedback system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the GrowthPilot dashboard and all its features.
  • To track your habits, roadmap progress, and learning activities across sessions.
  • To process premium subscriptions and manage your account status.
  • To send important notifications about your account, subscription expiry, or service updates.
  • To respond to your inquiries submitted through our Contact Us form.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our features and user experience.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity or unauthorized access.

3. Data Storage & Security

GrowthPilot takes your data security seriously:

  • Local Storage: Your primary data (habits, schedule, roadmap progress, notes) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.
  • Encryption: Sensitive notes and personal data are protected with AES-256 encryption before storage.
  • Authentication: We support secure Google OAuth 2.0 sign-in and encrypted password storage.
  • No Server Storage: Currently, your data does not leave your device. In future versions with cloud sync, data will be transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with trusted third-party services to enhance functionality:

  • Google OAuth: For secure sign-in. When you use Google Sign-In, Google's Privacy Policy applies to the authentication process. We receive only your name and email address.
  • Razorpay: For payment processing. All payment transactions are encrypted and processed directly by Razorpay. We do not store your full payment instrument details. Razorpay is PCI-DSS compliant.
  • Chart.js: For rendering analytics charts. This library runs entirely in your browser; no data is transmitted to external servers.
  • Tailwind CSS (CDN): For styling. This is loaded from a CDN for performance. No user data is shared with the CDN provider.

5. Cookies & Tracking

GrowthPilot uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and maintaining your login session.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your dashboard preferences and settings.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do NOT use tracking cookies for advertising or behavioral profiling.
  • No Analytics Trackers: We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking technologies.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose information only:

  • When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users.
  • With your explicit consent for any other purpose.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: View all your stored data through the dashboard.
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Correction: Update your account information when needed.
  • Opt-out: Discontinue use of the service at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email or phone number provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

GrowthPilot is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the dashboard or via email. Your continued use of GrowthPilot after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
